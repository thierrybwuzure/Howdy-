About me

I discovered the joy of coding just last year, and it completely changed my perspective on what I wanted to do with my life. What started as curiosity quickly became a passion as I began teaching myself the fundamentals of web development.

Those early days of self-learning were challenging but incredibly rewarding—every small victory, from my first working function to building my first responsive page, fueled my determination to keep going. Now I'm taking my skills to the next level by enrolling in a comprehensive training course to become a full-stack developer, combining my self-taught foundation with structured, professional education.

My goal isn't just to build websites—it's to create meaningful digital solutions for a cause I deeply care about. I want to specialize in developing e-commerce platforms specifically for organic farms and sustainable food producers. I believe that organic farming is essential for our health and our planet, and I want to help these businesses thrive online while educating customers about the real benefits of choosing organic. It's about connecting people who grow food with care to people who want to eat with intention.

For me, coding is more than just a career path—it's a way to merge technology with purpose. I'm excited to build a future where my work helps make organic, sustainable food more accessible to everyone.
